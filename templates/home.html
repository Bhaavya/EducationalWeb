Welcome to EducationalWeb!

EducationalWeb offers an enhanced way of learning through course slides.

Demo

Watch this video to learn how to navigate EducationalWeb



Below are some features you can try using:

  • Choose a course using the drop-down list in the navigation bar
  • Choose a lecture using the drop-down list in the navigation bar
  • Sequentially navigate through the lectures/slides using the Next and Prev buttons at the bottom of a slide
  • Navigate through slides in the current lecture by clicking on the slide thumbnails in the left panel
  • Search for relevant slides using the search bar at the bottom of a slide
  • Navigate to a related/recommend slide from the column on the right
  • Watch the lecture video by click on the Lecture Video button
  • Having difficulty understanding something on the slide? Find an explanation of a term/phrase on the slide by highlighting it and then clicking on the "cap/scholar" button on the top-right of a slide. It will try to retrieve a relevant section from the course textbook that contains an explanation of the selected phrase
  • Tell us if you liked an explanation (or not) by clicking on the Helpful/Not Helpful buttons below the explanation


Want to learn more about this tool?

EducationalWeb is based on the following papers:
  • Web of Slides (ACM L@S 2019): Details on the method used for identifying related/recommended slides
  • WOSView demo (ACM L@S 2019): Demo paper of the system
  • Explanation Mining:Details on the method used to retrieve explanations
